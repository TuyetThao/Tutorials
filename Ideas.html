PROJECT IDEAS


Overview


This is your first time come to Vietnam? Google Maps just only shows you some popular place such as famous church, museum, or famous shopping mall which are well known, these suggestions are quite boring because it completely does not describe life in the local area, the nooks and crannies where these local people often gather, where cultural dishes that famous landmarks may not be able to bring you - a tourist.


JustGo will be the perfect web app which will go with you everywhere, anytime you need. JustGo will give suggestions about places to eat, to visit, places that are said to be secretly containing the "soul" of that locality. All are recommended by reviews of locals and other travelers including prices, the service, the weather, ATM, medical store, hospital, public toilets, etc. Because of the flexibility, enhancing the user experience, the quintessential things may not be discovered and felt, but thanks to the reviews, tourists can also enjoy the beauty and taste of this place. In a way indigenous people enjoy it, this sense of familiarity has made JustGo stand out among other mapping applications.


Language is also one of the obstacles for tourists, but don't worry JustGo has provided Image Recognition feature, just take a photo of the place or food ... all information will be provided fully. (price, service, food, ...). Besides, visitors can also learn more about the history, the process of forming a place to visit, or derive from the dishes without having to have a tour guide, JustGo will provide the history of they also add to the deepening tourist experience.


Motivation


When traveling, what I have always wondered about is which place is fun, what is interesting, what restaurant is good, what is the price, what is the service? This is what motivates me to come up with the idea of creating JustGo. Vietnam's tourism industry is one of the potential industries that will bring a lot of profits to the country. Vietnam National Administration of Tourism reported that 2019 is a breakthrough in the tourism industry, Vietnam increasingly affirms itself with friends around the world through positive numbers like in 2019, Vietnam has welcomed 18 million foreign tourists which is the highest number recorded to date. Domestically, the number of tourists reaches 85 million from provinces. The tourism industry alone contributed 9.2% of the GDP growth when the revenue reached $ 32.6 billion. Although the growth is very positive, the tourism industry in Vietnam still has a number of hurdles, JustGo wants to connect tourists with local businesses, giving the most affordable suggestions match with personal preferences as well as implement a win-win model when both visitors and businesses benefit from connecting with each other which creates value for JustGo's platform.


Description


Review and rating feture

This is considered one of the features that JustGo is most interested in when introducing to users because this is the feature that JustGo leads when pioneering this feature on the basis of a navigation map. This feature allows users to truly reflect their experiences and directly rate the venue according to their satisfaction level. These reviews help to meet the needs to inquire first to have a brief idea of the service before they experience it. In addition, business owners can also know the level of customer satisfaction, which in turn improves their business.

User Log In/ Registration

This is one of the features that assess the success of a project. As more people sign up for an account, we will collect more data, increasing user interaction. This feature gives users a lot of benefits such as being notified of new places, promotions and offers to be sent to the user's account. This feature allows users to save login accounts without logging in multiple times. The most important thing is that when the user provides information to register the account, the user's password will be encrypted by encrypting algorithm for complete security when not even the data administrators can know the password of the users.

Seach Filter function

Personalize your travel experience as potential features gain the help of AI. Servers can store their visits and preferences to find the most relevant search results, save time as well as provide user satisfaction. The search section will be divided into many categories such as brand name, product, price, to suit the needs of customers. In addition, this tool also helps businesses to reach the right audience, maximize revenue and interest.

Business Advertisment

This is a feature that helps JustGo earn income to maintain and develop this platform, as well as businesses benefit from it. To appear on our advertisements, businesses need to contact to register and provide information including: service, price, location, phone number, JustGo's mission is to provide reputation. items that match the business's ad and help them reach more visitors whether or not they're searching for their information.

Display Business Information

In order for tourists to find and use products, businesses need to provide complete contact information, addresses, services, etc. Based on that information, JustGo can divide groups into different categories such as district 1, district 2, district 3 or food, drink, etc. It helps to simplify the search process that motivates customers to put through choice.

Chatbox

This feature is also supported by AI to provide customers with information 24/7 when they need assistance, AI also helps the server to filter information and recommend for users. Save staff costs when you do not need to hire staff to answer customers 24/7 directly. In addition, AI helps to improve communication language, make chatbox smarter, naturalize and diversify languages when supporting customers.


Tools and Technologies


Python Flask is used to build the web app, Figma is used to design the user inteface, thereby creating CRUD development, Nodejs and Reactjs used in the interface development of the website, MongoDB is used to manage the database, Postman to check CRUD functions, to stored the images we used Cloudinary, for map interface design we choose Google Map API, besides Git and bash are used in writing script to run on server AWS.


Skills Required


Proficiency in using MERN (mongodb, express, reactjs, nodejs) is required to use these programming languages to develop web apps. In addition, team work is an important skill when everyone's opinions are agreed and have the same goal to make this project a reality. Time management skill is required to complete all the deadline, tasks are equally divided and assigned to the right strengths of each team member to help the work flow becomes smoother.


Outcome


JustGo aims to provide a professional and user-friendly web app for customers between the ages of 18 and 50. JustGo provides not only convenient travel maps, but also detailed location suggestions. Local attractions and dining that other platforms do not offer. JustGo's goal is to contribute to positive growth in the development of Vietnam's tourism industry. Small / large businesses can also boost sales based on their attraction on JustGo. As a marketer in the future, my team and I can also rediscover their income through collaborating with businesses so they can attrack to their target customers. Besides, there are many ways to attract visitors and earn more revenue, such as: reviewing for receiving 10% voucher for the next time, sharing with friends to get 10% voucher, etc. At the same time, the economic development also brings many job opportunities for local people, since then the unemployment problem is also solved.
